Introduction

Growing up, I curiously witnessed technology evolve. And this evolution changed my life experience, little by little. Computers continually integrated something new and magical.

For many years I pondered this magic.

As I studied on a laptop while completing a degree in psychology.

As I worked with different information systems, as a data clerk.

As I streamed media, browsed websites, and interacted with apps.

Until I studied computer science and escaped the loop of curiosity.
